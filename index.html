Hello!

Welcome! I’m Ashley Haack, owner of Ashley Haack Fine Art Photography. I’m located in the panhandle of Nebraska, where I raise my four incredible kids with my husband Adam by my side.

My office — while unconventional — is not just my home, but it’s the forest, lakes, rivers and anywhere else I want to get lost. My expertise and love of photography has come from years and years of practice and figuring out what makes my creativity blossom. I love landscape photography, conceptual art, and photographing families and connections.

I grew up in between small towns and big cities, always ready for the next adventure. I’m introverted and mostly quiet. I love music and small moments. My biggest hope is to show the world “I was here.”

What is lifestyle photography? Lifestyle is the moments between poses. It’s the smiles, the kisses, the laughter, and sometimes the tears. The little hands reaching out for yours, the embraces that makes your stomach flutter. It’s all the moments that you don’t want to forget. It’s you, it’s them, it’s your life captured in the most beautiful way.
